Chickpea Curry Recipe

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Gather canned chickpeas, diced tomatoes, onion, garlic, ginger, coconut milk, and curry spices (e.g., cumin, turmeric, coriander, garam masala).
  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a large pan, heat oil and sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add Spices: Stir in curry spices and cook for a minute to release their flavors.
  4. Cook Tomatoes: Add diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and become saucy.
  5. Add Chickpeas and Coconut Milk: Stir in drained chickpeas and coconut milk. Simmer for 15-20 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  6. Season and Serve: Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
