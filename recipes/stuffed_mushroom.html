Spinach and Feta Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare Mushrooms: Clean mushroom caps and remove stems. Chop the stems finely.
  3. Prepare Filling: Sauté chopped mushroom stems with garlic and onions until softened. Mix with fresh spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and breadcrumbs.
  4. Stuff Mushrooms: Fill each mushroom cap with the spinach and feta mixture.
  5. Bake: Arrange mushrooms on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the topping is golden.
  6. Serve: Serve warm as an appetizer or side dish.
