Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Peppers: Cut the tops off bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes. Blanch them in boiling water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  3. Prepare Filling: Cook quinoa or rice according to package instructions. Mix with sautéed onions, garlic, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and cheese.
  4. Stuff Peppers: Fill each pepper with the mixture and place them upright in a baking dish.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until peppers are tender.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and serve warm.
